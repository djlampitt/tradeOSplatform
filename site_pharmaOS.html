The Problem

Pharma facility compliance is expensive, fragmented, and manually intensive

🗂

No single view of compliance across sites

Site managers use email, spreadsheets, and siloed CMMS to track deviations. One missed CAPA costs more than the platform. HQ has no real-time visibility until the weekly report arrives — by which time the deviation has compounded.

⏱

Field team deployment is reactive

Teams dispatch when things break, not when cGMP signals say they're about to break. Every day a cleanroom is offline costs $2,000+ in downtime — and manual scheduling means the right credential never arrives with the right person.

📋

Qualification paperwork is a bottleneck

IQ/OQ/PQ protocols, weld maps, MTR packages, and FDA documentation are assembled manually by QA staff. One missing document delays a product release. One out-of-date weld map triggers a 483 observation.

Platform Capabilities

Capabilities purpose-built for pharmaceutical operations

👁
PharmaWeldEye

ASME BPE orbital weld inspection: enforces SF1/SF4/SF6 Ra surface finish limits per WFI, PW, and CIP service. Deviation opened automatically on rejection. Weld maps generated and stored for FDA audit trail.

⚡
PharmaFaultLine

Fault triage at $145/hr pharma labor rate, $2,000/hr cleanroom downtime. cGMP deviations opened on critical events. Always requires cGMP clearance on work orders — no work proceeds without it.

👥
PharmaCrewMatch

cGMP clearance is a hard gate — never stretched. BPE_QUAL required for WFI orbital work. Facility Manager notified on every dispatch. Credential gaps trigger contractor search automatically.

🔗
PharmaChainSight

MTR verification against ASME BPE / ASTM chemical limits for 316L. Lot genealogy traces a bad heat number to every weld it touched across all facilities. Supplier risk flagged before parts ship.

🎓
PharmaSkillForge

6-month BPE requalification cycles. 2-coupon orbital weld qualification programs. 12-month cGMP annual training expiry — surfaced before crew dispatch, not discovered on-site during a 483.

🛡
PharmaPilotCheck

IQ/OQ/PQ acceptance always requires QA Manager co-sign — regardless of AI competency. FDA documentation release requires VP Quality sign-off. Regulatory co-sign, not just a competency gate. Capital spend logged with approver ID and timestamp.

📊
PharmaProjectPilot

IQ → OQ → PQ phase tracking with FDA documentation package assembly. Three feasibility approaches evaluated against timeline, cost, FDA risk, and crew availability before work starts. Board-level capital gates enforced.

🏭
FabQueue

NEW: HQ fabrication queue for weld spools, 3D-printed fittings, and CNC parts dispatched to facilities with MTR documentation. MTR hold enforced before shipping. Staged delivery schedules coordinated with facility timelines.

🧠
PharmaSpine

The integration backbone: Deviation, FabOrder, FacilityProfile, and MTRPackage entities. 11 pharma-specific event topics so a weld rejection cascades to crew, supply, training, and QA automatically — no manual handoffs.

Regulatory Framework

Every regulatory requirement, built in

Not bolted on after the fact. Each model is built with the regulatory requirement as a first-class constraint — not an afterthought.

✓ ASME BPE 2022
Orbital Weld Qualification

Orbital weld acceptance criteria enforced by PharmaWeldEye. Ra limits per SF category (SF1: 0.38 µm, SF4: 0.51 µm, SF6: 0.76 µm). ID surface finish measured and recorded. Weld maps generated automatically and stored with lot and coupon traceability.

✓ FDA 21 CFR 211 / Part 11
Electronic Records & Audit Trail

Electronic records with complete audit trail for every AI recommendation — logged with worker competency level, model version, and timestamp. Capital approvals logged with approver ID and timestamp. Part 11-compliant e-signature flow for QA and VP Quality authorization.

✓ cGMP / ICH Q10
Deviation & CAPA Management

Deviation management from detection to CAPA to closure — tracked in PharmaSpine with full event history. No work is performed without cGMP clearance verified at dispatch. Annual training expiry surfaced before crew deployment, not during an inspection.

✓ IQ/OQ/PQ · ISPE / GAMP 5
Qualification Phase Tracking

Phase tracking with gate events. QA Manager co-sign required at each IQ, OQ, and PQ phase. FDA documentation package assembled from weld maps, MTR index, and qualification protocols — ready for submission without manual assembly by QA staff.
